Welcome to my homepage.

I'm a post-doc at J. Mike Walker '66 Department of Mechanical Engineering, Texas A&M University, College Station, TX. I'm now advised by Profs. J N Reddy and Arun R Srinivasa. I got my PhD from Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore, India, Prof. Debasish Roy was my PhD advisor.

My research lies at the interface between geometry and mechanics. I often write computer codes to gain insight into problems. Check out my publications to know more about my research.

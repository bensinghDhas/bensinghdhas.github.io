Hi, Welcome to my homepage.

I'm a final (fifth) year PhD student at the Department of Civil Engineering, Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore, India.

Most of my research is towards modelling inelasticity in solids. Recently, we were trying to use tools from geometry to model inelasticity. I often write computer codes to gain insight about problems. Check out my publication to know more about my research.
